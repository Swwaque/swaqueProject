HURACÁN NUMBER 20,000
MARKS MILESTONE

9 May 2022

number 20,000 has just rolled off Automobili Lamborghini’s production line, marking a point in history for the iconic aspirated V10 engine. The model is a Huracán STO in Grigio Acheso Matt, and it’s headed to the Principality of Monaco, where it’ll bring many thrills to its new owner.

Lamborghini has reaped the benefits of eight
solid years of successes with the Huracán
family, starting with the original Huracán
Coupé (LP 610-4) in 2014 and culminating
with the most recent jewel, Huracán Tecnica.



“The Huracán has delivered an evolution of
design, technological know-how, driving
adventures, track records and sales records
since its launch,” says Stephan Winkelmann,
Chairman and CEO of Automobili
Lamborghini. “It was designed as a car to
provide super sports emotion in every
environment, from daily driving to thrilling
performance on track. Every iteration of the
Huracán has stayed true to that Lamborghini
philosophy.”

Huracán #20,000 is certainly a milestone worth celebrating.
